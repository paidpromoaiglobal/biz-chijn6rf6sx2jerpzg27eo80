Our Story

Made with Passion

Ashwin Super Market, located at 176 Yio Chu Kang Road, Singapore, is your go-to neighbourhood grocery store offering a wide selection of fresh produce, everyday essentials, and specialty items at affordable prices. We take pride in being a community-first store where every customer is treated like family, and convenience is always our priority.

From our kind-hearted owner who goes the extra mile to make customers feel valued, to our friendly and helpful staff, every visit to Ashwin Super Market is a warm and welcoming experience. We also offer free home delivery services, making it easier than ever to get what you need — right at your doorstep. Open seven days a week from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, we are always here when you need us.
